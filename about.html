About Me


I was licensed in July of 2000. I went out on my own as a solo operator in February of 2002.

I married Laura Barron in 1995 and we have both lived in McNairy County all of our lives. We have 4 boys that we have raised here as well.

My work consists of boundary and topographic surveys. I do farm surveys, subdivisions, and lot surveys. I also do construction staking and FEMA elevation certificates.
